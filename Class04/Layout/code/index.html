History about Coffee

Coffee's origin is uncertain, but it is believed to have originated in the ancient coffee forests on the Ethiopian plateau1. Legend says that the goat herder Kaldi first discovered the potential of these beloved beans1. Another drink that appeared around 1000 A.D. was made from the whole coffee fruit, including the beans and the hull2. It wasn’t until the 13th century that people began to roast coffee beans, the first step in the process of making coffee as we know it today2. The word “coffee” has roots in several languages.
